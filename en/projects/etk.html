Goals:

To make the best website in Kazakhstan. With such a complex request we have started our work on this project. After going through the technical requirements we have decided to take the challenge. We had to represent company’s services in the best light.

Process:

It was decided that the website needs to be dynamic to the maximum. Trendy parallax effects would work the best in this situation, that is why we took an advantage of this effect as much as we could. The system of railroad trains, logistics tanks and other equipments’ structures had to be researched and fully understood by our illustrators in order to perform the true and detailed picture of the logistics processes. Besides the desktop version of the website, we have developed the light mobile version for an easy access.
