Textile federation online marketplace
Goals:

Creation of a single-purposed platform with the several functions of an online shop for fabric designers and their customers. Young and professional designers from all over the world had to receive the opportunity to market their designs and products for the direct sales.

Process:

Since the main products are print designs and fabrics, the concept of the whole website design should’ve been simple and restrained. At the same time, the functionality of the international project had to be full and big enough to serve many international customers. In order to optimize the process of sales we had to review many types of online payment systems and sales patterns to achieve the best user experience.
