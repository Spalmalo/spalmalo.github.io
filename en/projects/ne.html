Web service and mobile application
Online radio ne.fm
Goals:

Our client had the best DJ’s in Kazakhstan, cool music and the desire to give the audience something better than just a radio. There were supposed to be 3 types of content: audio, video and text. Our main task was to create an easy access to these content to the fullest. At home, in the car, at your office - people had to have an opportunity to use NE.FM and use it in a very accessible way.

Process:

Website and mobile applications on iOS and Android platforms were developed by one of our teams. Despite the fact that we had an extensive experience in development, we had to dive deep into specifics of a radio studio’s everyday work, learned the radio ethernet and streaming servers control tools. We learned about Icecast from totally other side, and also plunged into the specifics of media content delivery using cellular networks.
