Megacom official website
Goals:

Our customer “Megacom” - is one of the largest mobile operators in Kyrgyzstan. We had an opportunity to totally reconstruct the website of the company: beginning from design to its functionality. One of the biggest issues that we had during the process was the combination of large amount of information and functionality with its modern, laconic and “light” design.

Process:

We have completely changed the structure and design of the website, focusing on the latest company information and the most necessary functions for customers. Two versions of website were launched: desktop and mobile. With this order, we have received an excellent experience with a large and structured company, where the approval and test stages were the major part of the workflow.
