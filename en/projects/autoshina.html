Web development
Online shop autoshina.kg
Goals:

One of the largest automobile tires supplier in Bishkek decided to make their web site even more convenient for customers and employers. There were three challenges in the project: re-design, content update and order procedure simplification.

Process:

Laconism, handy functionality and modern design were the project priorities. The accents was made on the goods. The search engine was extended, which made it more useful for the new customers. We added the price calculator and the possibility to process orders from the cart skipping registration at the web site.
